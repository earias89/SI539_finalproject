Hi! I'm Erika.

Social Scientist, Reproductive Rights Advocate, & FemTech Enthusiast

I am currently a second year Social Demography PhD trainee at Michigan's Population Studies Center. I am also earning my Master of Science in Information, with a specialization in agile development, data analytics, and UX research. My research interests lie at the intersections of relationship formation and online dating markets, reproductive autonomy, and reproductive intentions and decision-making among men, women, and couples, with a particular focus on voluntary childlessness.
