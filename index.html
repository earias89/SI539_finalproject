Hello! I'm Erika.

Social Scientist, Reproductive Rights Advocate, & FemTech Enthusiast

Erika is a social demography predoctoral trainee at Michigan's Population Studies Center. Her research interests lie at the intersections of relationship formation and online dating markets, reproductive autonomy, and reproductive intentions and decision-making among men, women, and couples, with a particular focus on voluntary childlessness.
